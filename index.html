It's a simple way to make beautiful stuff

Embroidery is the craft of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn.
It may also incorporate other materials such as pearls, beads, quills, and sequins.
In modern days, embroidery is usually seen on caps, hats, coats, blankets, dress shirts, denim, dresses, stockings, and golf shirts.
Embroidery is available with a wide variety of thread or yarn colour.
It's an easy skill to learn.
